Our Process: From Milk to Ghee

We believe in purity, not shortcuts. Our process is simple and traditional. First, we collect the freshest, high-quality cow's milk. We then churn this milk to create rich, wholesome butter. Finally, the butter is slowly simmered to perfection, clarifying it into the golden, aromatic, and pure desi ghee you receive.
