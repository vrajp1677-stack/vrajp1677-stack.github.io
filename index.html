Our Process: From Farm to Jar

1. The Source

It all begins with our healthy, free-grazing Desi cows. We collect their fresh, nutrient-rich milk every morning.

2. The Cream (Malai)

The fresh milk is gently processed to separate the thickest, richest, and most nutritious part: the cream, or *malai*.

3. The Churn

This pure, golden cream is then traditionally churned to create wholesome, delicious, fresh butter (*makkhan*).

4. The Simmer

The butter is slow-simmered in a heavy *kadai* to clarify, allowing milk solids to separate and develop a rich, nutty aroma.

5. The Nectar

The result: pure, golden, granulated ghee. Filtered and bottled with zero additives. Nothing added, nothing taken away.
